About Netflix

At Netflix, we believe in the power of storytelling to bring people together. Our mission is to entertain the world by delivering a diverse and compelling array of content that caters to every taste and preference. From gripping dramas and side-splitting comedies to groundbreaking documentaries and immersive interactive experiences, Netflix is your one-stop destination for all things entertainment.

With a presence in over 190 countries, we are committed to bringing the best stories from around the globe to your screen. Our vast library spans multiple genres, languages, and cultures, reflecting the rich tapestry of human experiences.
